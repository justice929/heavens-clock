Privacy Policy

Heaven's Clock is built around a simple promise: we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

Last updated: [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

1. Who we are

Heaven's Clock is developed and published by justice ("we", "us"), based in [COUNTRY]. You can reach us anytime at paxpax13@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

None on our servers. Heaven's Clock has no user accounts, no backend database, and no analytics SDKs.

The following information stays only on your device (the app's local storage) and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Birth date and optional birth time you enter during onboarding
  • Hoped-for lifespan in years
  • Device time zone (read locally, not sent)
  • Selected theme, mood, and language
  • Bucket-list items you create
  • Premium entitlement flags after a successful purchase

Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

3. Permissions

  • Internet — required for Google Play in-app purchases and OS/app updates. The app itself never sends your personal data over the internet.
  • Google Play Billing — used only when you tap a purchase or restore button.

The app does not request location, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, SMS, or photo permissions.

4. Payments

Yearly Premium and Lifetime Premium purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Google is the merchant of record and handles your payment information directly. We only receive:

  • An order confirmation (anonymous order ID, product ID, purchase state)
  • Permission to grant your device the premium features

We never see your card number, address, full name, or Google account email. Google's handling of payment data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Third-party services

ServicePurposePersonal data we receive
Google Play BillingProcess in-app purchasesNone (only order metadata)

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, AdMob, or any other tracking or advertising SDK.

6. Children

Heaven's Clock is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has stored information through the app, contact us and we will help you delete it.

7. Your rights

Because all personal data stays on your device, you can exercise your rights at any time:

  • Access / Correct — open the app's settings (⚙) and edit your information.
  • Delete — uninstall the app, or clear all fields from the onboarding edit screen.
  • Withdraw consent — uninstall the app.

If you live in the EU/UK (GDPR), Korea (PIPA), California (CCPA), or another jurisdiction with similar rights, the above applies. We have no server-side data to provide, correct, or erase.

8. Data retention

Data lives on your device until you uninstall the app or clear it manually. We have no retention obligation because we hold nothing centrally.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever start collecting data on our servers, we will update this page and notify users inside the app before the change takes effect.

10. Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests: paxpax13@gmail.com
