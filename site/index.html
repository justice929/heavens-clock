The app

A clock for finite days.

Heaven's Clock is not a productivity dashboard. It is a ritual: mortality becomes attention, attention becomes action, and action becomes a life you recognize.

Life Clock

See your remaining life as years, days, hours, minutes, seconds, and living rings.

Today's Bucket

Let one meaningful bucket item rotate on the main screen so it stays close to your choices.

Clock Skins

Premium clock skins can make the same reminder feel ancient, luxurious, futuristic, or deeply personal.
