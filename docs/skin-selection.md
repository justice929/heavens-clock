# Clock Skin Selection

## First App Skin

- Selected skin: Hologram
- Asset: `assets/skins/hologram-clock.png`
- Reason:
  - Clean circular composition
  - Works well behind the existing app rings
  - Low text pollution compared with several other generations
  - Strong premium/future impression

## Next Preview Candidate

- Candidate: Zen Stone Garden
- Asset: `assets/skins/zen-clock-preview.png`
- Reason:
  - Calm and distinctive
  - Strong fit for the Memento Mori mood
  - Better as a separate calm skin than as the first high-impact launch skin

## Red Team Notes

- Avoid images with visible fake text or large numbers when using them inside the app.
- App UI should render all real numbers and labels directly.
- 1:1 assets work best as clock-face skins.
- 9:16 assets work best as store screenshots, landing hero images, or full-screen promotional backgrounds.
