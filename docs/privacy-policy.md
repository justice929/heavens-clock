## English

**Last updated: 2026-06-01**

This policy explains how the **Heaven's Clock** mobile app (and the related website) handles your data.

### 1. Who we are
Heaven's Clock is developed and published by **justice** ("we", "us"), based in Republic of Korea.
You can reach us at **paxpax13@gmail.com**.

### 2. What data we collect
**We do not collect any personal data on our servers.** Heaven's Clock has no user accounts, no backend database, and no analytics SDKs.

The following information is stored **only on your device** (in the app's local storage) and never leaves it:

- Birth date and (optional) birth time you enter during onboarding
- Hoped-for lifespan (years)
- Time zone (read from the device, never transmitted)
- Selected theme, mood, and language
- Bucket list items you create
- Premium entitlement flags after a successful purchase

Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

### 3. Permissions and why they are used
- **Internet** — required for Google Play in-app purchases and standard app/OS updates. The app itself does not send your personal data over the internet.
- **Google Play Billing** — used only when you tap a purchase or restore button.

The app does not request location, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, SMS, or photos.

### 4. Payments
Purchases (Yearly Premium, Lifetime Premium) are processed by **Google Play Billing**. Google is the merchant of record and handles your payment information directly. We only receive:
- An order confirmation (anonymized order ID, product ID, purchase state)
- The right to grant your device the premium features

We never see your card number, address, full name, or Google account email.

Google's handling of your payment data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 5. Third-party services
| Service | Purpose | Personal data we receive |
|---------|---------|--------------------------|
| Google Play Billing | Process in-app purchases | None (only order metadata) |
| Capacitor runtime | App framework | None |

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, AdMob, or any other tracking SDK.

### 6. Children
Heaven's Clock is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us and we will delete it from any device we are aware of.

### 7. Your rights
Because all personal data stays on your device, you can exercise your rights at any time by:
- **Access / Correct** — Open the app's settings (⚙) and edit your information.
- **Delete** — Uninstall the app, or use Onboarding "Edit" → clear all fields.
- **Withdraw consent** — Uninstall the app.

If you live in the EU/UK (GDPR), Korea (PIPA), California (CCPA), or another jurisdiction with similar rights, the above applies. We have no server-side data to give, correct, or delete.

### 8. Data retention
Data lives on your device until you uninstall the app or clear it manually. We have no retention obligation because we hold nothing centrally.

### 9. Changes to this policy
If we ever start collecting data on our servers, we will update this page and notify users inside the app before the change takes effect.

### 10. Contact
Questions, concerns, or requests: **paxpax13@gmail.com**
